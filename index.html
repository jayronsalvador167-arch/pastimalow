Our Story

Pastimallow was born from a passion for creating sweet moments and sharing joy through delicious treats. Our founder, a pastry chef with over 10 years of experience, wanted to reinvent the classic marshmallow by infusing it with unique flavors and a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture.

We carefully source our ingredients and craft each Pastimallow by hand to ensure the highest quality and most delightful experience. Our mission is to bring a little sweetness to your everyday life with our fluffy, flavorful marshmallows.

Whether you're enjoying them with your morning coffee, as a dessert after dinner, or sharing them with friends, Pastimallows are the perfect treat for any occasion.
